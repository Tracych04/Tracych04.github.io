Major Projects

Midterm Project

For our midterm project, we designed a one-page website on a topic of our choice. I chose the US Wolf Refuge, a sanctuary for wolves and wolf-dog hybrids located north of Reno, Nevada. The page highlights the mission and funding, and there are photos of some of the current residents. Three versions of the design were created as part of the project: a desktop,tablet, and mobile device.
